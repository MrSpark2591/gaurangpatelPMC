Our Awesome Story

Gaurang Patel PMC® started out as a small firm that has grown exponentially by accepting every challenge that came its way. Our initial success came from designing each and every project in Gandhinagar, our base. Thereafter, we have not looked back by aiming ever higher.

Today our pallet of work covers everything from a small residence to corporate offices and school to huge commercial complexes, from an individual shop to large commercial projects. But the driving force behind the success has always been the openness to continuous learning, the sheer joy and pride in our work, and embracing change as the only constant in life
